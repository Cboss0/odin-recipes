Edible Spaghetti and Meatballs

Sorry this is going to be lazy making really good marinara and meatballs is alot of work and I just want to get this assigment done. So we will be using pre made everything but pasta. It will still be good and to be honest learning al dente noodles is the first real step so not all will be wasted.

Ingredients

  • Preggo Marinara Sauce
  • Barillo Spaghetti
  • Store Pre cooked meatballs

Instructions

  1. First we need a large pot and a medium sauce pan. Fill the large pot to just above half full with water and set burner to high.

  2. Then in the medium sauce pan fill 3/4 full of preggo sauce and set burner to low medium. With the intent to start a light simmer on the sauce.

  3. When the water comes to a boil add a tablespoon of salt to the water then take 300g of spaghetti and drop in. Using a pair of tongs stir and twist the spaghetti until it's fully submerged.

  4. When sauce is simmering, add pre cooked meatballs and let them soak in the sauce. Don't let it "boil".

  5. After about 10mins of boiling the spaghetti take a strand out with a fork and taste test it. We want the noodle to have no crunch or resistance, keep testing the noodle until it bites soft. This usually takes 10-15min depending on elevation and noodle thickness.

  6. When the spaghetti is ready pour threw a strainer to drain all the water and place on plate or bowl. Then scoop meatballs out of sauce and place on noodles. Add sauce untli lightly coating all noodle.
