Baked Potatoe

So somebody is trying to teach the youths that microwaving and eating a potatoe raw is ok. So lets just learn how to properly bake in an oven the most basic of potatoe.

Ingredients

  • Medium Russet Potatoe
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter

Prep and Cook

  1. Set the oven to 400F and get a baking pan out line with foil or oven safe parchment paper.

  2. Take the potatoe, with a fork deeply stab 3 or more times in different locations making sure to atleast penetrate to the core.

  3. Coat potatoe in oil and rub salt into skin

  4. Place potatoe on baking pan and place on the middle rack of the oven.

  5. Kick back and wait. Depending on the size of the potatoe it should take 30-1hr to fully cook. Easy way to check is after 30-40 min with the fork stab into potatoe, when it gives no resistance all the way it is finished.

  6. When potatoe is soft remove from oven and with your hands push down on both sides of the potatoe splitting it open. Add Butter salt and pepper and enjoy.
